TechFest 26

About Event

TechFest 2026 is an annual college festival that brings students to participate in technical, cultural, and fun activities.

Event Schedule

  1. Opening Ceremony - 9:00 AM
  2. Coding Competition - 10:00 AM
  3. Technical Quiz - 12:00 PM
  4. Cultural Performances - 2:00 PM
  5. Prize Distribution - 5:00 PM

Rules

  • Participants must carry their college ID card.
  • Registration is mandatory.
  • Maintain discipline during the event.
  • Follow the instructions of event coordinators.
  • Any form of indiscipline will lead to disqualification.
